Opis posla

Responsibilities:


• Build up and maintain a knowledge of the Marvelsoft software, configuration files, integration, and business flows to assist in issue investigation
• Be involved in application system checks, identifying and resolving issues before they impact our customers (first-line support)
• Escalate issues to team leaders, business, and management as appropriate
• Attend, and coordinate technical sessions with current and potential clients
• Respond to client email and phone requests
• Investigate and resolve reported issues
• Prioritize, log, and keep issues up to date on the issues tracking system

Kvalifikacije

Requirements:


• Effective in a team, able to develop and maintain good relationships to complete tasks successfully
• Excellent communication skills and customer focus
• Ability to create and maintain strong working relationships both with customers and internally
• Desire to learn both technical and business aspects of customer workflows and the broad range of functions delivered across all aspects of Marvelsoft software
• Ability to troubleshoot and approach problem-solving in a logical manner
• Ability to communicate confidently and concisely using business acumen
• Computer Science or IT-related degree
• Technical knowledge or experience in the following areas:
– Linux (CentOS or Redhat Enterprise preferred)
– One or more programming/scripting languages (e.g., Linux Shell script, Visual Basic, C#)
– Time-series databases (preferably InfluxDB)
• Excellent written and spoken communication skills in English

Dodatne Informacije

Appreciated skills:

• Familiarity with the global marketplace and trading sector
• Familiarity with FIX protocol
Marvelsoft is a company that values diversity and is always searching for individuals who are passionate, creative, and talented, with the skills and experience to help clients overcome their business and regulatory challenges. We are looking for a collaborative team player who can successfully build and deliver valuable products. If this sounds like you, we strongly encourage you to apply.
All interested candidates should send their CVs or resumes in English to careers@marvelsoft.net by June 30th.
