O kompaniji

QSS is focused on delivering high-quality cloud services in our modern data centers, software development solutions, and system integrations enabling our clients to improve and secure their business, with our innovative IT solutions deployed on clients' premises or in our Cloud.

QSS provides Software development solutions in Fintech, Telco, IoT, Content management solutions (CMS), Business Analytics solutions, including AI, ML etc.

In its Cloud, QSS offers a broad spectrum of cloud services, enabling each client to decide whether to use IaaS, PaaS, or SaaS.

Opis posla

Are you a skilled and experienced .Net Full Stack Developer looking for an exciting new challenge? Do you want to work on cutting-edge software solutions that have a real impact on people's lives? If so, we have the perfect opportunity for you!

We are seeking an experienced and motivated .Net Full Stack Developer to join our dynamic team. As a member of our team, you will work on a wide range of challenging projects using the latest .Net technology stack. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams of designers, developers, and project managers to deliver innovative software solutions that exceed customer expectations.

Our ideal candidate is already experienced working with .Net technology stack and has a passion for delivering high-quality software solutions. You possess a strong understanding of software development principles and methodologies, and you have experience with front-end frameworks such as Angular or React.
You will be in charge of:
● Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, develop, and implement software solutions using the .Net technology stack
● Develop and maintain web applications using ASP.Net, C#, JavaScript, and SQL Server
● Participate in code reviews, testing, and debugging of applications
● Optimize application performance and scalability
● Write clean, maintainable, and reusable code that adheres to industry standards and best practices
● Stay up-to-date with emerging trends and technologies in software development

Kvalifikacije

● Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field
● Minimum of 2 years of experience in software development using the .Net technology stack
● Proficiency in ASP.Net, C#, JavaScript, and SQL Server
● Experience with front-end frameworks such as Angular or React
● Strong understanding of software development principles and methodologies
● Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills and ability to work in a fast-paced, team-oriented environment
● Strong communication and interpersonal skills
